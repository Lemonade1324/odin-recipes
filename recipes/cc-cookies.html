Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Want cookies that melt in your mouth? Where each bite is filled with chocolatey goodness? You've come to the right place!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 3 cups plain flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Make sure the butter is softened and eggs are room temp.
  2. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  3. Beat butter and both sugars with an electric mixer in a large bown until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time then stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, then add to the batter along with the salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough, 2 inches apart, onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake until edges are slightly browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Once out, leave on baking sheet for a few minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
