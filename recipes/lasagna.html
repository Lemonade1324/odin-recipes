World's Best Lasagna

Description

This recipe is sure to make your tastebuds tingle and warm you up in the cold winter months!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16 oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste along with water. Season with sugar, half the parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel, and pepper. Cover and simmer for about an hour and a half, stirring occasionally.
  3. Cook lasagna sheets in a pot of lightly salted, boiling water for 8-10 minutes or according to package instructions. Once done, drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, remaning parsley and a bit of salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C.
  6. Assemble lasagna in meat sauce, ricotta, lasgna sheets order. On top layer, sprinkle parmesan along with slices mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for a further 25 minutes.
  8. Rest for 15 minutes before serving.
