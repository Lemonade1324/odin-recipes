Corn Fritters

Description

The absolute best corn fritters you will ever have. Crisp golden outside with an amazingly soft, moist inside.

Ingredients

  • Oil (for frying)
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp shortening, melted
  • 1 can corn

Steps

  1. Heat oil until 185 degrees C.
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a medium bown. BEat egg, milk, and melted shortening together in a small bowl. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture then add in corn.
  3. Drop batter by the spoonful into the hot oil and fry until golden brown. Be careful not to overcrowd the pan.
  4. Remove fritters with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
